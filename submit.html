Submission Guidelines

Manuscript Preparation

  • Format: PDF or Word document
  • Length: No strict limit, but typically 3,000-8,000 words for research articles
  • Language: English (we provide language editing support if needed)
  • Structure: Title, Abstract, Keywords, Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion, Conclusion, References
  • References: APA, IEEE, or field-specific format
  • Figures/Tables: High resolution, properly labeled

Required Information

  • Complete author information with affiliations
  • Corresponding author contact details
  • Abstract (250-300 words maximum)
  • 5-10 keywords
  • Conflict of interest statement
  • Ethical approval information (if applicable)
  • Data availability statement
