About Syntavion Journal

Founded by Dr. Angela Rossi

Syntavion Journal was founded in 2025 by Dr. Angela Rossi, Computer Scientist and Engineer, with a vision to democratize scientific publishing and make cutting-edge research accessible to all. Dr. Rossi's extensive experience in academia and industry has shaped the journal's commitment to excellence, innovation, and open science.

Our Mission

Syntavion Journal is dedicated to advancing scientific knowledge across all disciplines through open access publishing. We believe that scientific progress is accelerated when research is freely available to all, breaking down barriers that have traditionally limited access to cutting-edge discoveries.

Our Vision

To become a leading platform for interdisciplinary scientific communication, fostering collaboration between researchers from diverse fields and promoting innovative approaches to solving global challenges.

Scope and Coverage

We welcome submissions from all scientific disciplines, including but not limited to:

  • Physical Sciences (Physics, Chemistry, Earth Sciences)
  • Life Sciences (Biology, Medicine, Ecology)
  • Mathematical Sciences
  • Computer Science and Engineering
  • Environmental Sciences
  • Social Sciences with quantitative approaches
  • Interdisciplinary and emerging fields

Peer Review Process

All submissions undergo rigorous peer review by experts in the relevant field. Our review process is designed to be:

  • Fast: Initial decisions within 4 weeks
  • Fair: Double-blind review when requested
  • Constructive: Detailed feedback for improvement
  • Transparent: Clear criteria and expectations

Publication Ethics

Syntavion Journal adheres to the highest standards of publication ethics, following guidelines from the Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE). We are committed to:

  • Ensuring originality and preventing plagiarism
  • Maintaining confidentiality during review
  • Addressing conflicts of interest transparently
  • Supporting responsible research practices
  • Promoting diversity and inclusion in scientific publishing

Copyright and Licensing

All articles published in Syntavion Journal are made available under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License (CC BY 4.0), allowing for maximum reuse and distribution while ensuring proper attribution to authors.
